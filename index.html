The Security House Model

Think of your organisation as a building — every entry point matters. Security is not just the front door (website). It's the windows, back door, keys, rooms, safe, alarm, and fire escape working together.

⚡

Attackers Scan Everything

Automated tools probe every exposed asset 24×7. A single unpatched server, a legacy VPN with no MFA, or one unaware employee is all it takes. Simple controls eliminate the majority of real-world risk.

Framework

The Six Pillars of Organisational Security

A strong security posture requires all six pillars working in concert.

Pillar 01

Network Security

Firewalls, segmentation, and traffic controls — managing what enters and exits.

Pillar 02

Endpoint Security

Device hardening, patch management, and EDR — protecting every device.

Pillar 03

Identity & Access

MFA, least-privilege access, and regular access reviews.

Pillar 04

Data Protection

Encryption, DLP controls, and secure backup policies.

Pillar 05

Operational Process

Security policies, awareness training, and incident response plans.

Pillar 06

Resilience & BCP

Tested DR plans, failover systems, and business continuity workflows.
